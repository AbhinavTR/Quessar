🚀 Why We Built Quessar™

In a city that never sleeps, EVs were supposed to be silent — but their charging problems are anything but quiet.

Public charging stations are often:

  • Crowded or broken
  • Noisy with generators
  • Inconveniently located

We started Quessar™ to fix this.

Our goal is simple:
Bring clean, quiet power to your EV — wherever you are.

Whether you're at home, at work, or parked on the street, Quessar™ dispatches a silent mobile charging unit directly to you. It’s quiet, efficient, and contactless.

We’re a proudly Indian startup:

  • 🚀 Recognized by Startup India
  • ✅ UDYAM Registered
  • 🔒 Provisional Patent Filed

We're launching in select areas soon — starting with Bangalore.

If you're an EV rider, delivery partner, fleet operator, or investor interested in supporting India's clean mobility future — we’d love to connect.

🔧 Behind Quessar™: The Tech & The Team

At Quessar™, our mission is powered by technology that's simple for the user, yet sophisticated behind the scenes.

  • Mobile Modular Chargers: Our silent chargers are compact, fast, and designed for doorstep delivery.
  • Smart Dispatch System: Requests are routed to the nearest available unit with real-time tracking.
  • Battery Management: Each charge is optimized based on your vehicle type, battery health, and local grid status.
  • Noise-Free Operation: We use ultra-quiet components to keep your surroundings peaceful.

We're working on app integration, predictive maintenance, and expanding compatibility with all major EVs.

Quessar™ is founded by passionate engineers and clean energy advocates who believe India's EV revolution should be accessible and elegant.

  • Advisors – Our extended circle includes experts from renewable energy, logistics, and electric vehicle ecosystems.

Together, we're building a future where charging is not a stop — it's just part of the journey.

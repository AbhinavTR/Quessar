About Quessar

 

Quessar is a silent door-to-door EV charging service using mobile modular battery units. We aim to solve the inconvenience of public charging stations in busy cities by delivering power directly to your location.

 

Founded in 2025, Quessar is:

 
       
  • Recognized under the Startup India program
    •    
  • A registered sole proprietorship via UDYAM
    •    
  • Trademark filed and provisional patent granted
    •  
 

We envision a future where EV charging is as easy as ordering food — silent, fast, and mobile.

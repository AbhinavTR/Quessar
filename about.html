About Quessar

Quessar is a silent, door-to-door EV charging service that delivers clean power directly to your electric vehicle — wherever you are. Whether you're parked at home, at the office, or in a basement, we make EV charging effortless and quiet.

We use mobile, modular charging units to bring energy to 2W, 3W, and 4W EVs without the need for fixed infrastructure or loud generators.

Why Quessar Exists

EV adoption in India is growing fast, but public charging is still inconvenient, noisy, and limited. Quessar solves this by offering:

  • On-demand mobile charging at your doorstep
  • Silent operation for residential and office environments
  • Quick response, no waiting at charging stations

Our Vision

We believe EV charging should be as easy as ordering food — fast, silent, and mobile. Our mission is to power India’s EV future with convenience and care.
